FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your first order.

How do I place an order?
Browse our collections, find what you like, and send us a WhatsApp message with the item details or a screenshot. We'll confirm availability, size options and pricing within hours.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept PayPal, Zelle, Wise, Western Union, and other methods. Ask us and we'll find the most convenient option for your country.
How long does shipping take?
Delivery typically takes 10–30 days depending on your country and shipping method. We provide full tracking once your order is dispatched.
Do you ship worldwide?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries including the USA, UK, Europe, Middle East, Australia and more. Contact us if you're unsure about your location.
How do I know the quality is good?
Every order includes a QC video inspection. We can also arrange a live video call so you can see the item yourself before it ships. You approve — then we send.
Can I see more products not shown on the website?
Absolutely. Our full catalogue is much larger than what's shown here. Message us with any brand, model or style and we'll check availability for you.
What if I'm not happy with my order?
Your satisfaction is our priority. We handle every issue personally and work to find the best solution. We build long-term relationships — not one-time transactions.
How do I contact you?
WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us: +86 152 0400 2312. We're available 7 days a week and respond quickly to all enquiries.
